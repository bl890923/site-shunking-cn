It has an annual output of 12000 tons of various yarns, 20 million meters of textile and garment fabrics, and 300000 beds of cotton wadding products. The products are mainly exported to Southeast Asia, Africa and Latin American countries through middlemen, and the domestic sales network covers major and medium-sized cities in central and eastern China. In Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Fujian, Guangdong and other coastal markets have a high visibility, is inspection free products.

The company mainly engaged in cotton, chemical fiber textile processing; cotton, chemical fiber products, clothing processing, manufacturing, sales; textile raw materials import, textile, clothing import and export trade.

The company adheres to the quality concept of survival by quality and development by reputation. In 1999, the company passed the ISO9002:1994 quality system certification of China Quality Certification Center (CQC), and again passed the ISO9001:2008 quality management system audit and acceptance in 2009. It has the right to import and export textile raw materials and products. The company's "silver waterfall" brand grey cloth has been awarded the title of "famous trademark of Sichuan Province".
